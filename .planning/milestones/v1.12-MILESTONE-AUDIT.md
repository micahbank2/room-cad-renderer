- **`loadSnapshot` is now async** (Pattern A: async pre-pass before Immer `produce()`). This is a project-wide refactor that all future phases inherit — any new caller of `loadSnapshot` needs `await`.
- **Snapshot version 2 → 3.** Future migrations chain off this; v1→v2→v3 still works.
- **Single source of truth keyboard registry** at `src/lib/shortcuts.ts`. Coverage-gate test prevents drift.
- **Direct `map={tex}` prop pattern** standardized for R3F texture rendering. Reusable for future texture-mounting work.
