# v1.9 Polish & Feedback — Milestone Archive

**Shipped:** 2026-04-25 (single-day milestone)
**Goal:** Close v1.8 carry-over tech debt, gather real-use feedback to inform v2.0 scoping, and ship the bug-fix actions that emerge from the feedback signal.

**Source:** v1.8 audit AUDIT-01 (formal VERIFICATION.md backfill), Phase 39 real-use signal gathering, [GH #96](https://github.com/micahbank2/room-cad-renderer/issues/96) per-surface `tileSizeFt` isolation bug.

## The story

v1.9 was originally scoped as a 4-phase milestone: doc backfill (Phase 38), feedback session (Phase 39), ceiling resize handles (Phase 40 / CEIL-01), per-surface texture tile-size override (Phase 41 / TILE-01). The milestone had an explicit hedge: "Phases 40-41 explicitly subject to Phase 39 reordering."

Phase 39's feedback signal — gathered via async 5-question questionnaire (CONTEXT D-08 pivot from in-person hybrid due to calendar constraints) — surfaced **zero friction reported** and **zero new wishes** beyond the GH backlog. Jessica's answers contradicted the hypotheses behind both Phase 40 (ceilings "went fine") and Phase 41 (texture sizing "feels right").

The milestone honored its own hedge: Phase 40 + Phase 41 were CANCELLED mid-milestone and re-deferred to backlog (Phase 999.1 + Phase 999.3). The narrower Phase 42 (BUG-01 — fix [GH #96](https://github.com/micahbank2/room-cad-renderer/issues/96) per-surface `tileSizeFt` isolation) was added as the v1.9 closer because the bug exists regardless of feedback signal.

**Net result:** smaller, more honest milestone than originally scoped. The mid-milestone re-scope is itself the most valuable artifact — it validated the "feedback-first" sequencing pattern by acting on it instead of overriding it.
