## Cross-Cutting Decisions

- **Evidence-driven prioritization** — only build items with real reports; park speculative items even if curated. Validated on Phase 39's 8-item v2.0-scope-seeds list (5 shipped, 6 deferred).
- **Honest deviation > fabricated implementation** — Phase 44 D-03 documented snap guides needing no guard rather than implementing a guard for a non-existent animation.
- **Token-level fixes over per-site edits** — Phase 43 UX-02 fixed 124+ `text-text-ghost` usages with one token bump rather than auditing each site.
- **Bundling by code-surface proximity** — Phase 43 packed 4 GH issues into 1 plan because all 4 touched adjacent files (Toolbar / PropertiesPanel / templates / design tokens). Atomic commits preserve 1:1 issue→commit mapping for clean closing references.
- **AUDIT-01 policy resolution** — substitute-evidence policy formalized globally during v1.10 audit. SUMMARY.md is canonical evidence; VERIFICATION.md is optional. Three milestones of de facto practice now codified.
