## Executive Summary

v1.10 was the smallest milestone in the project's history (2 phases, 2 plans, ~10 commits). It validated the **"evidence-driven prioritization"** pattern as a follow-up to v1.9's "feedback-first sequencing" — built only the 5 GH-tracked items with real evidence; deferred 6 speculative items (Pascal competitor-set + Properties-panel-in-3D + PBR extensions) to v1.11+.

The truthful finding ships better than the fabricated three-target implementation would have.

- 5 stale `[ ]` checkboxes (drift) — fixed inline
- A11Y-01 deviation (honest; documented; accept)

None block `/gsd:complete-milestone v1.10`. Recommend fixing AUDIT-02 inline (this audit) before milestone close.
