<task type="checkpoint:human-verify" gate="blocking">
  <name>Task 2: Manual SNAP-01/02/03 verification with Jessica</name>
  <what-built>
    Phase 30 smart snapping, fully wired:
    - Drag a product (or custom element, or ceiling) near a wall → edges snap flush; a purple axis guide line appears across the canvas; guide clears on release
    - Drag near a wall midpoint → object center auto-centers on wall; a small purple dot appears at the midpoint in addition to the axis line
    - Place a product via the productTool → smart snap active during hover and at click
    - Hold Alt/Option while dragging or placing → smart snap is suppressed (grid snap still applies if enabled)
  </what-built>
  <how-to-verify>
    1. Start dev server: `npm run dev`. Open the app.

    2. **Snap feels snappy, not laggy (Perf):**
       - Load / create a scene with ~20+ products and several walls.
       - Drag a product around for 10+ seconds near various walls and other products.
       - Confirm no perceptible lag. Guides appear/disappear responsively as you cross snap tolerances.

    3. **Guide line is readable on dark canvas (SNAP-03):**
       - Drag until a snap engages.
       - Confirm the accent-purple axis line is clearly visible against the obsidian background AND the grid pattern.
       - When midpoint snap engages, confirm the midpoint dot is visually distinct from the axis line.

    4. **Alt/Option disables smart snap (D-07):**
       - Start dragging a product near a wall — confirm snap engages + guide visible.
       - While still dragging, press and hold Alt (Windows) or Option (macOS).
       - Confirm the snap releases immediately, guides disappear, and the object now follows the cursor freely (grid-snapped if gridSnap > 0).
       - Release Alt → smart snap re-engages.

    5. **SNAP-01/02 feel right across angles (requirements):**
       - Test snap near a horizontal wall → X-axis guide should appear when aligning vertically.
       - Test snap near a vertical wall → Y-axis guide.
       - Test snap near a 45° wall → observe v1 endpoint-only behavior (documented limitation per RESEARCH Pattern 4). Acceptable if it snaps to the wall's start or end X/Y value; escalate if it feels broken.
       - Test midpoint auto-center on each wall type — confirm "this is centered on this wall" reads clearly.
       - Test dragging one product near another product's edge — confirm edge-to-edge snap with a guide.

    6. **Guide cleanup (Pitfall 2 + 3):**
       - Drag, release mouse → guides disappear immediately.
       - Start a drag, then press V (switch to select) mid-drag → guides disappear.
       - Start a drag, then press W (switch to wall tool) mid-drag → guides disappear.
  </how-to-verify>
  <resume-signal>Type "approved" to continue, or describe any issue (which of the 6 checks failed, what you saw, any diagonal-wall or midpoint issue).</resume-signal>
